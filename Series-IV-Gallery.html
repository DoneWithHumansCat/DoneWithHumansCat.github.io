Veidt City

Veidt City

  Built and paid for by Edvikas Enterprises, Veidt City is the sprawling city located in the center of the LoserRoom.

  First founded by Slamen Schlong, who broke ground with the first of the Rising Sun Towers; Slamen abandonded the town shortly after in favour of moving north and founding the Avigon Empire. Edvikas Enterprises was founded soon after, and immediately began expansion.

Avigon

Avigon

  Founded by Slamen Schlong in the north, Avigon is shaded and controlled by the massive Cathedral which dominates the cities skyline.

  The city is a monument to extravagance, every building is planned meticulously, and every stone is carved and placed by hand. Avigon has been torn down and rebuilt twice, and yet is still the second largest city in the LoserRoom.
